Advanced Nuclear Systems

Fourth-generation nuclear reactors offer enhanced safety features, reduced waste production, and improved fuel efficiency. Small modular reactors (SMRs) provide flexible deployment options with significantly lower construction times and costs compared to traditional nuclear facilities.

Ammonia as Energy Carrier

Green ammonia is emerging as a versatile zero-carbon energy carrier and fuel. With established production infrastructure and high energy density, ammonia can be used directly in fuel cells or combustion engines, or easily converted back to hydrogen.
